Palace

Palace is a party game of deduction, investigation, and mystery solving.

  • This is how the games go:
    1. You are dealt a card with your role
    2. If you are the king, try to not reveal your identity, the beast is out to get you
    3. If you are one of the guards, the king's identity has been revealed to you, try to keep it secret
    4. If you are the beast, you can attempt to pounce on the king at any time, but then you will be revealed and detained
    5. At the end of two minute cycles, all players vote to kick a player out of the palace
    6. The palace team wins if they kick the beast out of the palace
    7. The beast wins if they have defeated the king
